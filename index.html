Who are we?








We are an F1 in Schools team based in Qatar, and we attend Doha College. In essence, F1 in Schools is an international competition where teams from around the world design and race miniature Formula 1 cars, racing at a local competitions and building up to the World Finals.

Our team consists of five high school students, who are passionate about STEM, and Formula 1. From working on 3D design to creating social media pages, our team covers all bases. We are excited to grow and develop in this exciting field, and determined to improve our skills.
